## What this is

**Dabar Grammar English** is a reading-first site for people who study English beyond small talk — including **English majors** and graduate-level readers. Long-form explanations live in **MDX** in the repository, with full articles in **English, Portuguese, and Spanish** (not UI i18n strings).

## The name _dabar_

In Biblical Hebrew, **דָּבָר** (_dabar_) often means **word**, **speech**, or **matter** — something said that carries weight. The project name nods to **grammar as careful attention to how words work**, without reducing language to drills.

## Who it is for

- Advanced learners who want **linguistics-flavoured** grammar, pragmatics, and syntax sketches.
- Students in **Letters / English** programmes who read in more than one language.
- Anyone who prefers a **calm, book-like** layout for long reading sessions.
