## You are welcome here

Dabar Grammar English grows better with readers who **correct typos**, **propose articles**, **improve translations**, or **open issues** when something is unclear. You do not need to be a professional linguist to contribute.

## What you can contribute

- **New or revised articles** (grammar, syntax, pragmatics, academic English, etc.).
- **Corrections** to existing MDX in any locale (`en`, `pt`, `es`).
- **Issues** on GitHub for bugs, broken links, or suggestions.
- **Discussion** before a big change — open an issue first if you prefer.

## Steps (short)

1. Visit the repository: **[github.com/jhonatasfender/dabar-grammar-english](https://github.com/jhonatasfender/dabar-grammar-english)**.
2. For bugs and ideas, use **[Issues](https://github.com/jhonatasfender/dabar-grammar-english/issues)**.
3. For how we work (branching, MDX frontmatter, review): read **[CONTRIBUTING.md](https://github.com/jhonatasfender/dabar-grammar-english/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md)** on GitHub.
4. Submit changes via **pull request** when you can; maintainers will review.

Thank you for helping keep advanced English study **accurate, readable, and multilingual**.
