## No change of meaning (many verbs)

_Start doing_ ~ _start to do_; _begin to do_ ~ _begin doing_ — register and rhythm differ slightly.

## Meaning shift pairs

- _stop smoking_ (cease) vs _stop to smoke_ (stop **in order to** smoke)
- _try fixing_ (experiment) vs _try to fix_ (endeavour)

## Bare infinitive environments

After **perception** (_see him leave_), **causative** (_make her redo_), and **modals** (_must leave_) — do not insert _to_.

## Gerund as subject

_**Reducing** bias **is** harder than **detecting** it._ — _-ing_ clause as NP; infinitive subjects are possible but stylistically marked: _**To err** is human._

## Five-minute practice

List ten verbs you use with _to_. Check each in a learner dictionary for **allowed patterns** (_V to_, _V -ing_, both).
