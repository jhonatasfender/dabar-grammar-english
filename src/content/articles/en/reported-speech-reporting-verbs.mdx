## that-clauses

- **Neutral:** _say, state, report, note_
- **Evaluative:** _claim, argue, insist, deny_ — carry **stance** toward the proposition.

## Verb + object + that

_tell, assure, inform, convince_: _She **informed** us **that** the trial **had** ended._

## Infinitive reporting (_verb + noun + to-infinitive_)

_Advise, order, remind, warn_: _They **warned** the team **not to** ship._

## Gerund patterns

Some verbs allow **-ing** for the reported act: _She **admitted** **making** an error._

## Academic hedging bundles

**Suggest** + that-clause often patterns with **modal** retention: _The results **suggest** that bias **may** remain._

## Quick checklist

1. Does your verb **license** _that_, **to**, or **-ing** for this meaning?
2. Does the verb signal **neutrality** or **evaluation**?
3. Have you avoided **preposition** errors (_said me that_)?
