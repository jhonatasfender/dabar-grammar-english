## Fixed frames

- _**listen** **to** the brief_
- _**depend** **on** the sample size_
- _**apologise** **for** the delay_

## Passives keep the preposition

_The team **was** **listened** **to** carefully._ (formal / rare but grammatical)

## Two-preposition verbs

_He **apologised** **to** us **for** the outage._ — each preposition marks a **distinct** role.

## -ing vs infinitive after preposition

After **prepositions**, use **-ing** (not _to-V_): _She’s keen **on** **improving** the pipeline._

## Quick checklist

1. Is the preposition **part of the lexical entry**, not free choice?
2. In passive, is the **preposition** still adjacent to the **verb**?
3. Have you avoided **L1 transfer** of preposition (_discuss about_)?
