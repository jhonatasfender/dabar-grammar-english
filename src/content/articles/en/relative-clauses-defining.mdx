## Restrictive function

_The **users who opted in** received the beta._ — only **those users**, not all users.

## Relativisers

- **who** — personal subjects: _the analyst **who** flagged the spike_
- **which** — non-personal, especially non-restricted style; **that** common in restrictive NPs.
- **whose** — possession: _a firm **whose** model leaked_

## Zero relative (object position)

_The file Ø we **uploaded** was corrupt._ — object gap **licensed** in restrictive clauses (register-sensitive).

## Preposition stranding vs pied piping

Formal: _the channel **in which** we broadcast_  
Informal: _the channel **which** we broadcast **in**_

## Commas are misleading signals

**No commas** for defining relatives in edited prose — commas mark **non-defining** instead.

## Quick checklist

1. Would removing the clause **widen** the denotation incorrectly?
2. Is **that** vs **which** aligned with your variety’s **house style**?
3. If the gap is **subject**, zero relative is **ungrammatical** in standard English.
