## Simple past: bounded events on the storyline

_She submitted the file at 11 pm._ — completed in the narrative frame.

## Past progressive: internal view of an ongoing situation

_She **was revising** the discussion when the server crashed._ — background **open** at crash time.

## Interruption pattern (classic but not exclusive)

While + past progressive + simple past: _While I **was running** the script, the kernel **threw** an error._

## Statives and progressive coercion

_**I was knowing** the answer_ is odd; _**I was thinking** of a workaround_ is fine—mental verbs split between state and process readings.

## Parallel progressive: simultaneous scenes

_While the team **was collecting** data, ethics **was reviewing** the protocol._ — two ongoing processes.

## Five-minute practice

Take a short lab anecdote; underline **mainline verbs** (simple) vs **scene-setting** verbs (progressive). Remove progressive where the predicate is stative.
