## although / though / even though

_Fees rose, **although** demand stayed flat._ — **finite** clause.

**Though** can be **final**: _Demand stayed flat, **though**._

## despite / in spite of + NP / -ing

_**Despite** the outage, we shipped._  
_**Despite** **being** short-staffed, we shipped._

**No** finite clause directly after **despite** (_despite we_ is non-standard).

## whereas / while

**While** may be **concessive** or **temporal** — ensure context disambiguates.

## Quick checklist

1. Is the complement **finite** (subordinator) or **nominal** (despite)?
2. Have you avoided _despite that_ where **although** fits?
3. Does **while** force a **time** reading you did not intend?
