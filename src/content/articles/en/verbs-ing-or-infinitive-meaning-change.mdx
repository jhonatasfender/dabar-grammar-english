## stop

- _Stop **talking**_ — cease an activity in progress.
- _Stop **to** talk_ — pause **in order to** talk.

## remember / forget

- _Remember **locking**_ — recall a past act.
- _Remember **to** lock_ — don’t fail future duty.

## try

- _Try **restarting**_ — test a method.
- _Try **to** restart_ — attempt (effort reading).

## regret

- _Regret **telling** him_ — sorrow about a **past** deed.
- _Regret **to** inform you_ — formal **bad news** formula (fixed).

## go on

- _Went on **reading**_ — continue same activity.
- _Went on **to** read_ — proceed **to a new** stage.

## Quick checklist

1. Does the paraphrase with **in order to** fit (**infinitive of purpose**)?
2. Does the complement denote a **completed** sub-event (**-ing**)?
3. Is the pattern **idiom** (regret to inform) — memorise as chunk?
