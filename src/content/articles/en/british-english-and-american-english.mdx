## Spelling systems

- _colour / color_, _centre / center_, _analyse / analyze_ (publisher-dependent **-ise/-ize** policies).

## Grammar snapshots

- **Collective nouns:** BrE often allows plural agreement with **team**; AmE tends toward singular.
- **have got** for possession: common in BrE; AmE prefers **have** alone in many contexts.
- **gotten** as AmE participle of _get_ vs BrE **got** in perfects.

## Lexical swaps

- _lift / elevator_, _flat / apartment_, _holiday / vacation_ — choose per **audience**.

## Punctuation

Quote integration and **comma** conventions with **which** clauses vary by **house style**, not national law.

## Quick checklist

1. Is spelling **consistent** (CMS / Oxford / Merriam)?
2. Have you avoided **mixed** automotive vocabulary in one paragraph?
3. Does your institution mandate **one** variety for exams?
