## _A_ vs _an_: next **sound**, not next letter

- _a university_ / _an hour_
- _an NLP pipeline_ (if read as _en-el-pee_ initial vowel sound)

## Indefinite article: instance or type

- **Token introduction:** _We trained **a** transformer model._
- **Type / role:** _She works as **a** reviewer for two journals._

## Definite article: shared identifiability

Use _the_ when the reader can **locate** the referent: anaphoric (_the model **we** just introduced_), uniqueness in frame (_the CEO_), or situational obviousness (_Close **the** window._).

## Coordinations

In _**a** knife and fork_, some speakers accept single article for stereotyped pairs; with two distinct items, two articles may be clearer: _a knife and **a** spoon_.

## Proper nouns (advanced sketch)

Geographical and institutional names vary idiosyncratically (_the United States_, _Lake Geneva_). When in doubt, check a **dictionary or institutional site**, not a single heuristic.

## Five-minute practice

Highlight every article in one paragraph of your draft; for each NP, label **first mention**, **anaphoric**, **generic**, or **unique**.
