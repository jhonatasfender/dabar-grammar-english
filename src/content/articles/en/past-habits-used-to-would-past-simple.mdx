## _Used to_ + infinitive

- Past habit: _We **used to** deploy on Fridays._
- Past state: _The API **used to** be synchronous._

Negation and questions use **did** periphrasis in modern English: _We **didn’t use to** run lint in CI._

## _Would_ for repeated past events

- Narrative iteration of **dynamic** situations: _Every winter, the river **would** freeze._

Avoid **_would_** with **stative** verbs for pure description (_would know_, _would live_) — prefer **used to** or **past simple**.

## Past simple + frequency adverb

- _I **walked** to campus **most days**._ (habitual reading comes from **adverbials**)

## Quick checklist

1. Is the predicate **dynamic** enough for narrative **_would_**?
2. Does the habit **contrast** with present reality (**used to**)?
3. Have you avoided **_would_** for **states**?
