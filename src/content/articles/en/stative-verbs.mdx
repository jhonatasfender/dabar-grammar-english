## Typical stative classes

- **Cognition:** _know, believe, doubt_
- **Emotion:** _love, prefer, hate_
- **Perception:** _see, hear, smell_ (when non-agentive)
- **Relation / possession:** _have, own, resemble, consist_

## Simple present alignment

_I **know** the answer._ — **state** holds across the interval of evaluation.

## Progressive coercion

- _I’m **seeing** the consultant tomorrow._ (**arranged** dynamic reading)
- _You’re **being** rude._ (**deliberate** behaviour)

## have

_I **have** a licence._ (possession) vs _I’m **having** trouble._ (**experiential** dynamic)

## smell, taste, feel

Ambiguous between **copular** (_The soup **tastes** good_) and **activity** (_She’s **tasting** the soup_).

## Quick checklist

1. Does progressive signal **temporary** or **performed** behaviour?
2. Is **have** **possession** or **event/experience**?
3. Have you avoided _I am knowing_ in **standard** English?
