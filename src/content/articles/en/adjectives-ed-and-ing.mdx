## Prototype contrast

- _The results **were surprising**._ (stimulus)
- _We **were surprised** **by** the effect size._ (experiencer)

## Preposition hooks

_Surprised **at/by/about**_, _interested **in**_, _concerned **about/with**_ — check frames; they do not always parallel between _-ed_ pairs.

## Not all pairs exist

_Boring/bored_, _exciting/excited_ — but some verbs lack one form as adjective; verify in corpus rather than coin ✱_funed_.

## Advanced: attributive restrictions

Some _-ing_ forms are fine attributively (_a **convincing** argument_); certain _-ed_ forms resist pre-noun position or need hyphenation in compounds—edit for naturalness, not morphology alone.

## Five-minute practice

Rewrite five sentences swapping _-ed_/_-ing_; mark where the **semantic role** breaks, not just “grammar”.
