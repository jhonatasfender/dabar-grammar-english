## so + adjective/adverb

_It was **so** **cold** the pipes froze._  
_She spoke **so** **clearly** everyone followed._

## such + noun phrase

- **Singular count:** _**such** **a** mess_
- **Plural / mass:** _**such** **noise**_, _**such** **ideas**_

## Correlative results

_so/such … that …_ expresses **extreme degree → consequence**.

## so much / so many

Quantified scales: _**so** **much** latency_ — **not** _such much_.

## Informal exclamatives

_So**?!**_ in dialogue — keep out of **formal** exposition.

## Quick checklist

1. After **such**, is there a **full NP** (determiner + head)?
2. Is **so** modifying **Adj/Adv**, not a bare noun?
3. Does **that** clearly introduce **result**, not purpose?
