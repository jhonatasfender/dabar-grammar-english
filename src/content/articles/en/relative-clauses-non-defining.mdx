## Supplementation, not restriction

_My supervisor**, who** joined last year**,** approved the leave._ — the head is **already singled out**.

## Commas or dashes

Set off the clause with **paired commas** or **dashes**; a **single** comma splice is an error.

## Relativiser preferences

- **who/whom** for people (_whom_ as object in formal registers: _Dr Lee**, whom** I met …_)
- **which** for non-personal heads — **that** is **avoided** in standard non-defining relatives.

## Sentential which

_The model failed**, which** surprised us._ — **which** scopes over the **whole prior clause**.

## Quick checklist

1. Is the head noun **already uniquely determined** without the clause?
2. Are delimiters **paired**?
3. Have you avoided **non-defining that** in careful prose?
