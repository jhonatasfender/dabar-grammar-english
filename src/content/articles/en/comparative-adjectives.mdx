## Synthetic vs periphrastic

- Short monosyllables / selected disyllables: _shorter_, _simpler_
- Longer / _-ful_ / _-ous_: _more careful_, _more robust_

Do not combine: ✱_more simpler_.

## _than_-clauses and ellipsis

_She is more cautious **than I am**._ — supply the missing auxiliary to avoid ambiguity.

## Non-gradable adjectives

_*More unique*_ is contested; prefer _more nearly unique_ or rephrase (_distinctive_, _rarer_).

## Correlative comparative

_**The** larger the sample, **the** narrower the interval._ — paired determiners + comparatives; do not mix word order from L1 calques.

## Five-minute practice

Take five claims from your discussion; rewrite each with an explicit **than**-standard or _as … as_ paraphrase to expose hidden assumptions.
