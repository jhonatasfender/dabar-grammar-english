## Boosters before comparatives

_much **shorter**_, _far **more** complex_, _a lot **older**_ — **very** is marginal here (_very taller_ is non-standard).

## Hedge modifiers

_slightly **better**_, _a bit **more** conservative_ — narrow the **gap**.

## as … as and not as/so … as

_She’s **almost** **as** **fast** **as** her teammate._  
_It’s **not nearly** **as** **bad** **as** we feared._

## the same as / similar to

_Equal**ity** of degree_ without canonical comparative morphology.

## Implicit standards

Comparatives presuppose a **reference**; make it **explicit** when precision matters (_than the baseline build_).

## Quick checklist

1. Is the modifier **licensed** before comparative adjectives/adverbs?
2. Have you avoided **double comparative** (_more better_)?
3. Is the **than**-constituent **parallel** in category?
