## Frames as mini-predicates

- _keen **on** + NP/ing_
- _responsible **for** + NP/ing_
- _consistent **with** + NP_

The preposition is part of the **construction**, not a free choice.

## Near-synonyms, different frames

_Angry **with** a person_ vs _angry **about** a situation_ — subtle stance distinctions; do not collapse to a single preposition.

## _to_ + infinitive vs _in_ + _-ing_

Some adjectives allow both with little change (_ready **to** go_ / _ready **for** the trip_); others are fixed: _likely **to**_, not ✱_likely for_.

## Academic bundles

_Conductive **to** bias_, _subject **to** approval_, _compatible **with** prior work_ — learn **NP-adjacent** bundles as wholes.

## Five-minute practice

Collect ten adjectives you overuse (_important_, _necessary_, _different_). For each, write the **licensed preposition frames** from a dictionary corpus line.
