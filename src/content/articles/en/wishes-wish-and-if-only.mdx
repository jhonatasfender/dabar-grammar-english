## Present counterfactual

_I **wish** I **knew** the schedule._ (I don’t know)

## Past regrets

_I **wish** I **had** **saved** the transcript._ — **past perfect** inside wish.

## Persistent annoyance (second clause)

_I **wish** he **would** stop interrupting._ — **would** targets **external** behaviour.

## if only

Stronger **emotive** frame with the **same** tense logic: _**If only** we **had** **branched** earlier._

## Quick checklist

1. Is the embedded tense **one step back** from reality time?
2. Is **would** signalling **volition** of another agent, not your own wish list?
3. Have you avoided _wish + would_ for **first-person** abilities you control?
