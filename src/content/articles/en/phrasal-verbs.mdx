## Transitive + separable

_Pick **up** the kids_ / _Pick the kids **up**_ / _Pick **them** **up**_ — pronoun **must** precede the particle.

## Intransitive or fixed order

_Look **after** the cat_ — **after** cannot **freely** migrate (_look the cat after_).

## Three-part phrasal verbs

_Cut **down** **on** sugar_ — treat as **holistic** idiom.

## Literal vs idiomatic

_Pour **out** the tea_ (spatial) vs _She **poured** **out** her feelings_ (idiom).

## Academic register

Heavy PV density can sound **informal**; sometimes **Latinate** one-word verbs fit better (_continue_ vs _carry on_) — match **genre**.

## Quick checklist

1. Is the object **pronoun** sandwiched **verb–pronoun–particle** when separable?
2. Is the cluster **three-part** — don’t split illegitimately.
3. Have you checked the **idiom** in a learner dictionary (not compositional)?
