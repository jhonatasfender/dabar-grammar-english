Para **sujeto vs objeto** (_I/me_, _they/them_), ver el artículo **object pronouns** (`object-pronouns`).

## Reflexivos

_She **taught** **herself** Python._

## Enfático

_I **myself** ran the benchmark._

## Preposicional

_He bought a ticket **for** **himself**._

## Fijos

- _**Behave** **yourself**._
