## stop

- _Stop **talking**_
- _Stop **to** talk_ — parar **para** hablar.

## remember / forget

- _Remember **locking**_
- _Remember **to** lock_

## try

- _Try **restarting**_
- _Try **to** restart_

## regret

- _Regret **telling** him_
- _Regret **to** inform you_

## go on

- _Went on **reading**_
- _Went on **to** read_
